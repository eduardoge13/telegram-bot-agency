Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

This policy explains how Blue Sky Travel collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you contact us or use our WhatsApp-based travel assistance service.

Information We Collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and profile name.
  • Messages, images, and other content you send to us.
  • Travel details you share, such as destination, dates, passenger count, and budget.
  • Contact details you provide so we can follow up on your request.
  • Technical data needed to deliver the service, such as timestamps and delivery status.

How We Use Information

  • To answer your questions and provide travel assistance.
  • To search, quote, and manage bookings or requests you ask us about.
  • To continue conversations and remember the context of your inquiry.
  • To maintain service quality, security, and troubleshooting logs.

Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share data with service providers that help us operate the WhatsApp automation, analytics, calendars, sheets, and booking workflows.
  • We may share travel-related details with suppliers or partners only when needed to respond to your request or complete a booking.

Retention

  • We keep conversation and service records only as long as needed to provide support, manage bookings, and meet operational or legal requirements.
  • You may request deletion or correction of your information where applicable.

Your Choices

  • You can stop messaging us at any time.
  • You can ask us to update or delete information we hold about you, subject to legal and operational limits.
  • If you do not want us to continue a conversation, reply with a request to stop.

Security

  • We use access controls and operational safeguards to protect your data.
  • We limit access to systems that process customer messages and travel requests.
